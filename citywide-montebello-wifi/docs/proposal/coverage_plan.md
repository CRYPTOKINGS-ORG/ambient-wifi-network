# Coverage Plan

## Residential Zones
Dense grid of outdoor APs on light poles spaced 200–400 ft apart.
Rooftop APs on city buildings and partner properties.

## Commercial Corridors
Whittier Blvd, Beverly Blvd, Montebello Town Center.
Directional APs for high‑traffic density.

## Civic Areas
City Hall, Library, Community Centers, Senior Centers.

## Parks
Montebello City Park, Grant Rea Park, Sanchez Adobe, Acuna Park.
Mesh APs for open‑area coverage.
